How it works

  1. Share your request using the form above. Share as much or as little as you’re comfortable with.
  2. We review daily. A real person on our team reads every submission.
  3. We pray within 24 hours. Your request is shared (confidentially) with our prayer team.
  4. Optional follow‑up. If you opted in, we’ll send a brief weekly email with encouragement.
